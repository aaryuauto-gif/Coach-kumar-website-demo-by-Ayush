iv. The Process

How Kumar coaches you.

Five phases. Each one designed to remove friction from your progress — so all you have to focus on is showing up and executing.

i.

Apply & Discovery Call

Submit your application, then jump on a free 20-minute call with Kumar. He'll assess goals, lifestyle, training history, and — most importantly — what's actually been holding you back until now.

ii.

Full Assessment & Baselines

A comprehensive body and movement assessment. Kumar defines clear, measurable targets and establishes baselines — so we can prove the progress instead of guessing at it.

iii.

Custom Program Design

Your bespoke training block and nutrition plan, engineered from scratch — no templates, no recycled spreadsheets. Built around your body, schedule, and specific objective.

iv.

Execute & Weekly Check-Ins

Start training with clarity. Weekly video check-ins keep you accountable, and Kumar recalibrates the plan based on real biofeedback and results — never guesswork.

v.

Transform & Peak

Deliver the physique you actually came here for — earned through precision, consistency, and elite-level coaching. Whether it's stage-ready or just the best version of you, we hit the target.
